Welcome to the official website for Jigga, Government name Denzel Lichaha, Born and raised in the capital, Windhoek, Namibia. Jigga is a producer and rap artist from the Zambezi region, formerly known as Caprivi.

Jigga started producing beats in 2017 after he was introduced to FL(fruity loops) by @Mugatsu_whk, government name Morne Spiegel. The passion grew on from then on. In 2019 he recorded his first song titled FIGURES which had featured Mugatsu_whk. His passion for music was passed on to him from his family which has a variety of artists and art lovers.

This is the official website for Jigga, where you'll be able to stream and download most of his favorite projects and also get to know the artist.
